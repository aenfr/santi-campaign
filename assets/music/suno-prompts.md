## How to Use in Suno

1. **Go to**: suno.ai or suno.com
2. **Click**: "Create"
3. **Enable**: "Custom Mode"
4. **Paste Style Prompt** in the "Style of Music" field
5. **Paste Lyrics** in the "Lyrics" field
6. **Generate** and pick the best version
7. **Download** and save to `assets/music/`
